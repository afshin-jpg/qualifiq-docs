Franchisor Journey

Lead to Sign — Full lifecycle from website visit through Franchise Agreement execution

Phase 1 — Acquisition & Onboarding
STEP 01
Land on QualifIQ Website
Franchisor discovers QualifIQ, learns about the platform value proposition and reviews pricing plans.
User Action
STEP 02
Sign Up & Authenticate
Clicks sign-up button, directed to sign-up page. Authenticates via Email, Google, or LinkedIn.
User ActionAuth Flow
STEP 03
Select Pricing Tier & Payment
Prompted to select a pricing tier. Completes payment. Redirected to tutorial video page upon success.
User ActionPayment
STEP 04
Watch Tutorial Video
Franchisor watches the platform tutorial to understand features and capabilities.
Platform
STEP 05
Complete Onboarding Flow
Answers onboarding questions and provides company details. Upon completion, directed to the QualifIQ platform dashboard.
User ActionOnboarding
Phase 2 — Lead Generation
STEP 06 · DECISION
Choose Lead Generation Method
Franchisor selects one of two paths to generate leads: Campaigns or Cold Leads.
Decision Point
STEP 06A · PATH A
Run Social Media Campaigns
Access the Campaigns section to create and run social media campaigns to generate leads.
CampaignUser Action
STEP 06B · PATH B
Browse Cold Leads
Navigate to Lead Generation → Cold Leads sub-section. Apply relevant filters to view cold leads.
Lead GenUser Action
Phase 3 — Vetting & Qualification
STEP 07
Vet Cold Leads
Navigate to Vetted Leads screen, click Vetting button. View vetted leads with tick marks and match scores. Click any lead to view their high-level profile.
User ActionAI Scoring
STEP 08
Qualify Selected Leads
Select leads, click Qualify button, redirected to Qualified Leads (QL) sub-section. Platform triggers qualification emails to selected leads. Leads who reply become QLs.
User ActionEmail TriggerPlatform
STEP 08A · ALTERNATIVE
Upload Campaign Respondents as QLs
Franchisor can manually upload leads who responded to campaigns into the QL sub-section, or the platform automatically adds campaign respondents as QLs.
User ActionAuto-import
Phase 4 — QL Engagement & Discovery
STEP 09
Direct QL to Short Form
Franchisor emails the QL, directing them to the short form on the Franchisor's website and asks them to fill it out.
EmailUser Action
STEP 10
QL Completes Short Form → Notification
QL fills out the short form. Franchisor is notified. If responses are favorable, the system auto-sends an email to QL with a link to join QualifIQ and set up a Discovery Call.
Auto EmailNotificationFavorable?
STEP 11
QL Joins Platform → Discovery Call
Franchisor is notified that QL has joined the platform. Discovery Call takes place between Franchisor and QL.
NotificationMeeting
STEP 12 · DECISION
Post-Discovery Call: Unlock Long Form
If Discovery Call goes well, Franchisor unlocks the long-form section for the QL on QualifIQ. QL is asked to upload documents for verification.
Decision PointUser ActionPlatform
Phase 5 — Verification, NDA & FDD
STEP 13
Document Verification
Franchisor verifies the QL's uploaded documents on the QualifIQ platform.
User ActionVerify
STEP 14
NDA Initiation & Signing
If Franchisor is convinced, QualifIQ platform initiates the NDA. QL is notified to sign the NDA on the platform.
Platform TriggerNotificationNDA
STEP 15
FDD Sent to QL
After NDA is signed, platform informs Franchisor and automatically sends FDD to QL for review with a 14-day grace period to review and sign.
Auto-send FDDNotification
STEP 16
FDD Discussion Call
Calendar is created for QL to schedule a call with the Franchisor to discuss the FDD.
CalendarMeeting
STEP 17
FDD Signing Reminders
Platform sends automated reminders on days 3, 7, 10, and 14. Platform informs Franchisor when QL signs the FDD.
Auto RemindersDays 3, 7, 10, 14
Phase 6 — Discovery Day & Franchise Agreement
STEP 18
Discovery Day Scheduling
When FDD is signed, platform auto-triggers a Discovery Day calendar for QL to pick an in-person meeting date or schedule a call with Franchisor to discuss next steps.
Auto TriggerDiscovery Day
STEP 19 · FINAL
🎉 Franchise Agreement Execution
After Discovery Day is completed and both parties are in agreement, the Franchisor uploads the Franchise Agreement document for QL to execute. Journey complete.
